Rules of game ->

This is 2 player ludo with no repeat turn on 6.

You have to click on 'ROLL!' to roll a dice.

Tou can take out pawn only if u got 6.

You cant kill your own pawn. There are no safe squares on board.

There is cheat box in top left corner, you can check there.

Once you kill opponents pawn it will return to its house.
